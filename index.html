Reniscience Education LLP is an education consulting firm born out of a desire to promote learning that is empowering, joyful and relevant to the 21st century learner. We work with schools and other educational organizations to provide expertise, interventions and solutions for teacher professional development, whole-school reform, educational leadership development, student learning and curriculum development. We operate by collaborating very closely with our partners, with on-going feedback and support through the needs-assessment, design, implementation and exit phases of every project. All of our efforts are driven by our core beliefs about teaching and learning and focused on our goal of promoting 21st century learning.

WHO ARE WE

Reniscience Education is co-founded by two passionate educators- Purvi Vora and Sangita Kapadia.

Hi! First of all, thank you for visiting our site! My name is Purvi and I am one of the co-founders of Reniscience Education. I studied education at Teachers College Columbia University in one of the most exciting cities in the world… and somehow managed to earn a PhD there.  Over the past twelve years, I have been fortunate enough to don many hats in this sector- teacher, school leader, researcher, coordinator, teacher trainer, entrepreneur and of course, life-long learner. My passion for my work, my constant gripe with carelessly thrown around phrases in our sector (think ‘high-quality education’ or ‘excellence in education’), my inspiring encounters with highly motivated teachers and leaders working in the most difficult conditions, my desire to do SOMETHING to bridge the gap between theory and practice and my expertise and experience in the field, along with the support of my family and a fantastic business partner, led to the birth of Reniscience Education. I am always excited to meet new people and hear your ideas. Feel free to drop us a line- to exchange ideas, connect with us, recruit our services or just to say hi We look forward to hearing from you!!

Hi there! My name is Sangita and I am cofounder of Reniscience Education, with Purvi. Teacher, student, entrepreneur and mum – these are the four words that define me best. After acquiring a Master’s degree in organic chemistry, I worked as a chemist in the industry for a few years. However, my zest for learning and teaching eventually led me to earn my B.Ed. from Mumbai University. Since then, I have taught in a private school as well as an NGO, cofounded an education NGO and as a part of it, run my own center for kids from highly impoverished and marginalized communities. I strive to go beyond the textbook and make science an exciting process of discovery for my students. My students are seldom found inside the classroom, and instead can be often seen collecting leaf samples in the nearby park, measuring shadows of objects or even admiring the rainbow colours of light on an oil leak from a car! A combination of Purvi's passion and energy, and my experience have brought us together to this wonderful destination called Reniscience Education. I am sure you will enjoy surfing our site. Please feel free to share your ideas, and we look forward to hearing your opinion and connecting with you!

“Not everybody's going to be an entrepreneur, but everybody should be financially literate. Financial literacy is a base requirement like spelling or reading or something of the sort that everybody should acquire at any early age. The financial habits you develop when you are young are going to go with you into your adulthood. But you can't be an entrepreneur unless you're financially literate.” – Warren Buffett


Inspired by Mr. Buffett and his ‘Secret Millionaire’s Club,’ Reniscience Education offers a semester long financial literacy program called Money$en$e for 7th and 8th standard students. This program has four modules that focus on:

1. Overview of money

2. Understanding personal finances

3. Business and entrepreneurship

4. Banking, credit cards and investing


Using a combination of field trips, project-based learning, videos, workbooks and several experiential activities, the lessons are specifically designed for this age group. Students are provided with an opportunity to analyze their own finances, create budgets, pitch a business idea to win seed funding from Reniscience Education and then implement their business ideas to earn profit. They visit the RBI monetary museum in Mumbai, interview their parents, apply math concepts learned in class, discuss big ideas like integrity and debt; in the process they develop key 21st century skills of critical thinking, collaboration, creativity and managing complexity.
